WELCOME TO DICE GAME

How to play

1. Objective: Guess the secret number correctly within a range of numbers.
2. Game Rounds: You have a total of 10 rounds to play.
3. Winning Condition: To win the game, you must successfully guess the number correctly in at least 3 out of the 10 rounds.
Game Rules:
In each round, you will be asked to guess a number within a specified range.
If your guessed number matches the secret number, you win that round.
The game will keep track of the number of rounds won.
Game Outcome:
Winning: If you win at least 3 rounds out of 10, you win the game!
Losing: If you win fewer than 3 rounds after 10 rounds of play, you lose the game
